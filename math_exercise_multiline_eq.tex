\begin{eqnarray}
(a+b)(a+b) & = & a^2+ab+ba+b^2 \nonumber\\
           & = & a^2+2ab+b^2
\end{eqnarray}

Another way out to not include numbers for equation is:

\begin{eqnarray*}
(a+b)(a+b) & = & a^2+ab+ba+b^2 \\
           & = & a^2+2ab+b^2
\end{eqnarray*}
